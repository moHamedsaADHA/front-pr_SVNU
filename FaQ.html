General Questions

Q: What is a digital library?

A digital library is an online platform that provides access to a collection of digital books, articles, and other resources that can be read or downloaded electronically.

Q: What was the first digital library created?

The first digital library is considered to be Project Gutenberg, launched in 1971, aiming to make literary works freely available in digital format.

Q: What distinguishes a digital library from a regular library?

A digital library is accessible online anytime and anywhere, doesn't require physical space, and often offers search features and formats like PDF or EPUB. Regular libraries rely on physical books and in-person visits.

About Library

Q: Is using the digital library free?

Yes, there are free books, but there may be some paid content or content that requires login.

Q: Can I use the digital library for free?

Absolutely. Many of our books and features are available without charge.

Q: Do you need to register an account to benefit from the library's services?

While some features are accessible without an account, registering allows you to save books, create reading lists, and access personalized recommendations.

Your Account

Q: How do I create a new account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button On the side of the page, then fill in your details and follow the instructions.

Q: Forgot my password, what should I do?

Click on "Forgot Password?" on the login page and follow the steps to reset your password via email.

Q: Can I edit my personal statements after registration?

Yes, you can update your profile information anytime from your account settings.

Q: Are there paid subscriptions and what benefits you provide?

Yes, we offer premium subscriptions that include early access to new books, ad-free reading, and exclusive content.

Q: How do I update my account information?

Go to your profile settings and click "Edit" to change your personal details.

Books and Reading

Q: Can I download books or just read them online?

Some books are available for download, while others can be read online only, depending on the licensing.

Q: How do I search for a specific book?

Use the search bar at the top of the site. You can filter results by title, author, or genre.

Q: Can I save books for later reading

Yes, just click the "Save" or "Add to Reading List" button on any book page.

Q: Do you offer books in different formats, such as PDF and EPUB?

Yes, many books are available in multiple formats like PDF, EPUB, and sometimes audio.

Q: How can I save books for later reading?

You can add them to your personal reading list by clicking the bookmark icon or "Save" button.

Q: Can I suggest adding a new book to the library?

Yes, we welcome suggestions. Use the "Suggest a Book" form in the contact section.

Technical problems

Q: What should I do if I have trouble downloading or reading a book?

First, try refreshing the page or using another browser. If the issue persists, contact support.

Q: What browsers and devices support the digital library?

The library works best on modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari, and is compatible with desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

Q: How can I report an error or issue on the website?

Use the “contact support” form at the bottom of the page or contact us via email.

Q: Why can't I open some books?

Some books might be restricted due to licensing or require a premium subscription to access.

Q: The website isn't working. What should I do?

Check your internet connection, clear your browser cache, or try again later. If the issue continues, contact technical support.

Copyright

Q: Can I share books with others?

Books from the public domain can be shared, but copyrighted materials should not be distributed without permission.

Q: Can I print books from the digital library?

Printing is allowed only for books that have print rights enabled. Please check the book's page for permissions.

Q: Are all available books legal and available for download?

Yes, we ensure that all content on the site respects copyright laws.

Q: How is copyright handled?

We comply with intellectual property regulations and only host or link to legally authorized content.

Q: What should I do if I find a book that violates intellectual property rights?

Please report it immediately through our "connect us" form. We take such concerns seriously.
